About Me

I was born and raised in a small town in Wyoming with a population of about 6,000 people. We had one high school in the town that had about 500 people and there were about 90 other students that graduated with me.

After high school, I attended the University of Wyoming where I graduate in 2006 with a degree in Mathematics and Math Education.

I have been teaching at East Bakersfield High School since August 2006.

Outside school, I have a wife and two children, both girls. We enjoy traveling. We went to Taiwan during the spring break of 2016. We frequently go down to L.A. and the beach. I also enjoy working on computers and learning how they work.
